Hello! I am currently a graduate student at the University of Southern California Keck School of Medicine (USC KSOM) pursuing a Master of Public Health (MPH), specializing in biostatistics-epidemiology; where I serve on the board of USC Iranian Graduate Students' Association (IGSA) which is a nonpolitical, nonprofit, volunteer student organization (established 1994); in order to foster friendship among different cultural groups, as well as provide a source of support for USC's Iranian/Persian community. I previously obtained a Master of Science in Global Medicine (clinical track) from USC KSOM on the Health Science Campus. Before that, I attended University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) where I received my Bachelor of Science in biochemistry with a minor in Iranian Studies.

In my spare time, I like reading books, playing soccer, visiting different beaches, as well as exploring the nature in Los Angeles.

My goal is to become a physician-scientist.
